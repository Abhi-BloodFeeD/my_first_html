LOGIC

Negation

Denial of truth of a sentence by asserting its Negation.
Ex:~
Sugar causes tooth decay.
Negation:~
~Sugar does not causes tooth decay

Conjuction

Use "and" to conjoin two sentences to make a single sentence is called Conjuction.
Ex:~
'Mary love John and John loves Mary' P & Q or P ^ Q

Disjunction

Disjunction is connection of two statement by "or" . The disjunction of two sentences is true if and only if at least one sentence is true.

Implication

Conditional sentence is false if and only if Antecedent(hypothesis) is true but consequent(conclusion) false.
Ex:~
If Mary loves John,then John loves Mary i.e A==>B
